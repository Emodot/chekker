About Us

Chekker is a digital health platform that offers quality at-home lab testing to every Nigerian. Chekker is helping patients get access to the best private laboratories from the comfort of their homes.

With Chekker, spending hours waiting in queues at diagnostic centers or hospitals is now a thing of the past, as you can now book their medical tests from the comfort of your home, get samples collected by the Chekker medical team, Lab results are well-vetted and interpreted by Medical professionals before they are sent to you within 24 - 48 hours.
